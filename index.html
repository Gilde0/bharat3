Frequently Asked Questions

How can I recognize gambling addiction?

Common signs include lying about gambling, chasing losses, financial problems, and feeling anxious or guilty after gambling.

Is there free help available for gambling problems?

Yes, many non-profits and health organizations offer free counseling, self-exclusion programs, and support groups.
